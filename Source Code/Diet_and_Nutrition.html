Food Essentials

The key to eating well is to enjoy a variety of nutritious foods from each of the Five Food Groups. Foods in the Five Food Groups are grouped by their type and their contribution of nutrients to diets. Within each group, healthy choices are those that are lowest in saturated fats, added sugars and added salt.
